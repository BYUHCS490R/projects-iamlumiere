Introduction:

Aloha Everyone! My name's Markham but people call me Jim. I live in the southern province of the Luzon island called Bicol and I live in Naga City, Camarines Sur in the Philippines. Our city has a tagline of Ang Maogmang Lugar which translates to The Happy Place due to it's welcoming and positive atmosphere. In addition to this, our city is also known for our campaign for tourism Naga Yon! which was derived from the bicolano word minagayon meaning to improve, to become better, or to progress.

As an individual, one of the best things that I've learned after my mission is learning to do things alone. To travel to unknown places just by myself, to be comfortable doing thing by myself, and to get used to just by being with myself. Probably because of how my mission stretched me to always go out of my comfort zone and to always act while in the growth zone despite the presence of fear. Thus, I always do things that makes me independent. Like going to a hike just by myself, swim to the beach, or watch a movie in a theater.
